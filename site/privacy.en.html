Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2024

1. Introduction

AfetNet ("we", "our" or "our app") is a mobile application that provides offline communication during disaster situations. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Required Data

  • Location Information: Approximate location for sending help requests (optional)
  • App Interactions: Help requests, resource sharing, and messaging data
  • Device Identifiers: Temporary device keys (not used for tracking)

2.2 Optional Data

  • Contact Information: Only when you send support requests
  • Telemetry: Anonymous usage statistics (only with consent)
  • Media: Photos for damage reports (compressed)

3. Data Storage

  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally on your device
  • 30-Day Retention: Data is automatically cleaned up after 30 days
  • Encryption: Sensitive notes are encrypted before storage
  • P2P Network: Messages are temporarily shared with other devices (limited by TTL)

4. Data Usage

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To relay help requests during disaster situations
  • To share emergency information via P2P mesh network
  • For experimental early warning system (early warning, not prediction)
  • To improve app performance (with anonymous telemetry)
  • To provide technical support

5. Data Sharing

  • Third-Party Sharing: Your data is not shared with third parties
  • P2P Network: Messages are only temporarily shared with nearby devices
  • Advertising: No advertising in the app
  • Sales: Your data is never sold

6. Security

  • Local Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted on your device
  • Temporary Keys: Temporary keys are used for P2P communication
  • Transit Encryption: P2P messages are encrypted in CBOR format
  • Data Integrity: Messages are verified with digital signatures

7. Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View your data through the Settings menu
  • Correction: Edit your data
  • Deletion: Delete all your data using "Delete All My Data" option
  • Export: Export your data in CSV/GeoJSON format
  • Telemetry: Opt out of anonymous telemetry in settings

8. Early Warning System (EEW)

Important Warning: The EEW system is an experimental feature:

  • This is an early warning system, not a prediction system
  • False alarms or missed warnings may occur
  • Always follow official sources like AFAD, Kandilli
  • Motion sensor data is used only for early warning
  • Data is anonymized and not used for tracking

9. Children's Privacy

AfetNet does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately.

10. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through in-app notifications. You can find the current version of the policy on this page.
