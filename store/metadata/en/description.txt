AfetNet is an offline-first emergency network app designed specifically for Istanbul. Communicate with other devices even when there's no internet connection.

FEATURES:
• Offline P2P Mesh Network: Communicate via Bluetooth with nearby devices
• Early Warning System: Earthquake P-wave detection and alerts
• Help Requests: Emergency assistance requests with location data
• Resource Sharing: Share water, food, shelter and other resources
• Family Communication: Secure communication with family members
• Offline Maps: Istanbul maps for offline use
• Damage Reports: Document damage with photos
• Volunteer Verification: QR code volunteer identity verification

SECURITY:
• All data encrypted
• Automatically deleted after 30 days
• No personal information collected
• Not shared with third parties

AfetNet saves lives by providing critical communication during disasters. Download now and be prepared!
